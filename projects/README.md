# Projects

Research-related side projects and one-off analyses.

**What goes here:**
- Quantitative screens and backtests
- Data collection projects (scraping, API integrations)
- Model-building experiments
- Collaboration projects with other analysts

**Distinction from Research/:** Research topics are ongoing analytical themes with wiki-style synthesis. Projects are discrete, time-bounded efforts that may feed into the wiki but have their own scope and deliverables.
