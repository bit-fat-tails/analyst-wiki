# Macro

Macro research and context that affects the coverage universe.

**What goes here:**
- Interest rate and monetary policy notes
- GDP, employment, inflation data and interpretation
- Geopolitical developments (trade policy, export controls, regulations)
- Cross-asset signals (FX, rates, commodities) relevant to your sectors
- Macro scenario analysis

**Tip:** Macro context is most useful when connected to your companies. After noting a macro development, ask Claude which company notes and concept pages should be updated. "Fed signals higher-for-longer" might touch every company with significant debt or capex sensitivity.
