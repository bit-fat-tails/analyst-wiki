# Meetings

Meeting prep and post-meeting summaries.

**File naming:** `YYYY-MM-DD-{slug}.md` (e.g., `2026-05-01-tsmc-management-call.md`)

**Typical content:**

**Pre-meeting:**
- Attendees and their roles
- Key questions to ask
- Relevant context from wiki (company notes, concept pages)
- Recent developments that should be discussed

**Post-meeting:**
- Key takeaways
- Action items
- What to update in the wiki (new signals for company notes, concept pages)

**Tip:** Ask Claude to prepare a meeting brief by pulling from company notes and concept pages. After the meeting, paste your notes and have Claude ingest them into the wiki.
