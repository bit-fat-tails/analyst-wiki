# Earnings

Earnings season tracking and analysis.

**What goes here:**
- Earnings calendar for your coverage universe
- Pre-earnings previews (expectations, key metrics to watch, consensus vs your view)
- Post-earnings reactions (beat/miss, guidance, market reaction, thesis impact)
- Cross-company earnings synthesis (what themes are emerging across reports)

**Tip:** Earnings season is the highest-velocity ingest period. Each report should trigger the full wiki workflow: update company notes, update relevant concept pages, update industry notes, log it. By the end of the season, your concept pages should reflect the cumulative signal across all reports.
